# Decision 001

Date: July 2026

Financial statement audits are excluded from OPAP.

Rationale:
OPAP focuses on performance audits that evaluate program effectiveness and produce findings and recommendations.

# Decision 003

# Decision 004

Corpus inclusion is determined by the content of the report rather than the dataset's Type field.

Rationale:
Historical metadata contains incomplete or inconsistent classifications.
